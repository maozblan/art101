/* list for repotting a plant */
1. Buy a separate larger pot/container for plant you want to repot
2. With the appropriate soil for plant, create a small soil base in the new pot for the plant
3. Carefully remove the plant from its current container
4. Place more soil in the new pot and create a supportive mound in the middle
5. Place the plant in the new pot in the center
6. Fill the pot with soil and secure plant
7. Water the plant 
8. Place in a spot with the right light and temperature conditions

						

/* list for rock paper scissors */
1. Find a friend
2. Count “rock”, “paper”, “scissors” 
3. Do a hand motion (rock, paper, or scissors) at the same time
4. Figure out who won
	a. Rock > scissors, scissors > paper, paper > rock
